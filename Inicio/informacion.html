Reglas fundamentales del past continuous y el when and while

Introducción al Past Continuous

El Past Continuous se usa para hablar de acciones que estaban en progreso en un momento específico del pasado.
Normalmente se combina con el Past Simple usando when (interrupción) o while (acciones simultáneas).

Los 5 conectores principales con Past Continuous

Estructura

was / were + verbo en -ing

SujetoAfirmativaNegativaPregunta
Iwas studyingwas not studyingWas I studying?
You/We/Theywere playingwere not playingWere you playing?
He/She/Itwas eatingwas not eatingWas he eating?

When y While

When → Acción corta interrumpe una larga (Past Simple + Past Continuous)
Ej.: I was cooking when the phone rang.

While → Dos acciones largas al mismo tiempo
Ej.: While I was cooking, my brother was watching TV.

Línea del tiempo: acción larga + interrupción

Ejemplos reales

  • I was sleeping when you called me.
  • While we were having dinner, it started to rain.
  • She was reading a book when the lights went out.
