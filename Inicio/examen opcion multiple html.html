Usos de When y While en Pasado Continuo

Responde las siguientes preguntas (escribe **when** o **while**) y haz clic en "Verificar Respuestas".

1. I was cooking dinner _____ the phone rang.

2. _____ I was studying, my friends were playing football.

3. I was sleeping _____ my mom arrived.

4. ¿Cuál de las siguientes frases es correcta? Escribe **solo** la frase correcta.

(a) While I was watched TV, my brother came.
(b) While I was watching TV, my brother was doing homework.
(c) When I watching TV, my brother came.

5. When I was walking to school, it started to rain. → ¿Cuál es el uso de "when" en este contexto? (Escribe **a**, **b** o **c**)

(b) Introduce una acción más corta que interrumpe otra.
