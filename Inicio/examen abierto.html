Usos de When y While en Past Continuous

Responde las siguientes preguntas (escribe **when** o **while** para las de llenar el espacio) y haz clic en "Verificar Respuestas".

1. I was cooking dinner _____ the phone rang.

2. _____ I was studying, my friends were playing football.

3. I was sleeping _____ my mom arrived.

4. ¿Cuál de las siguientes frases es correcta? Escribe **solo** la frase correcta.

(a) While I was watched TV, my brother came.
(b) While I was watching TV, my brother was doing homework.
(c) When I watching TV, my brother came.

5. When I was walking to school, it started to rain. → ¿Cuál es el uso de "when" en este contexto? (Escribe **a**, **b** o **c**)

(a) Introduce una acción más larga.
(b) Introduce una acción más corta que interrumpe otra.
(c) Significa lo mismo que "while".

6. They were talking _____ the teacher entered the room.

7. _____ my parents were driving, I was reading a book.

8. The light went out _____ we were watching the movie.

9. ¿Qué conector se usa a menudo para acciones simultáneas en pasado continuo? (Escribe **when** o **while**)

10. I was running **when** I fell down. → ¿Qué tiempo verbal sigue a "when" en este caso? (Escribe **a** o **b**)

(a) Pasado Continuo (Past Continuous).
(b) Pasado Simple (Simple Past).
