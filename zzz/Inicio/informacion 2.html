Past Continuous – ¡Lo Aprenderás Hoy para Siempre!

Past Continuous = "ESTABA HACIENDO" cuando pasó algo
was / were + verbo en -ing
AfirmativaNegativaPregunta
I was eatingI wasn't eatingWas I eating?
You were playingYou weren't playingWere you playing?

Los 4 Usos que Cubren el 99% de los Casos

1. Acción larga que fue INTERRUMPIDA
→ Usa WHEN + Past Simple

ESTABA HACIENDO → ¡PUM! pasó algo corto
Ejemplos:
• I was sleeping when the phone rang.
• She was driving when it started to rain.

¡Clásico!

2. Dos acciones largas AL MISMO TIEMPO
→ Usa WHILE (o AS)

ESTABA HACIENDO + ESTABA HACIENDO
Ejemplos:
• While I was cooking, he was watching TV.
• They were talking while we were eating.

Dos líneas en la línea del tiempo

3. Fondo de historia (set the scene)
→ ¡Para contar cuentos como los nativos!

It was raining heavily. The wind was blowing. I was walking alone in the dark street when suddenly…

¡Empieza así cualquier película de terror!

4. Acción repetida que MOLESTABA (¡el uso secreto!)
→ Con always / constantly / forever

He was always losing his keys! (¡Qué pesado!)
My neighbors were constantly playing loud music.

¡Para quejarte con estilo!

¡PROHIBIDO! Stative Verbs

NUNCA uses -ing con estos verbos:
know • like • love • hate • want • need • believe • understand • remember • forget • seem • appear • have (posesión) • own • belong • cost • weigh
✗ I was knowing the secret. → ¡HORROR!
✓ I knew the secret.

✗ She was having a big house. → ¡NO!
✓ She had a big house.

Past Simple vs Past Continuous

Past Simple = punto • Past Continuous = línea larga

15 Ejemplos Visuales (¡Pega tus fotos!)

I was sleeping when you called.

While I was studying, my dog was barking.

She was crying when I arrived.

The kids were playing while it was raining.

He was always complaining about everything!

At 10 PM yesterday, I was watching Netflix.

Past Continuous = ESTABA HACIENDO
→ Cuando pasó algo corto (when)
→ Mientras hacía otra cosa larga (while)
→ Para contar historias
→ Para quejarte con “always”
