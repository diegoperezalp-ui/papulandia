Conectores del past continuous y el when and while

Conectores más usados con Past Continuous

Cuando combinamos el Past Continuous con el Past Simple, usamos estos conectores para mostrar la relación entre las dos acciones.

Los 5 conectores principales con Past Continuous

1. WHEN → Acción corta interrumpe una larga
Ejemplo: I was having a shower when the doorbell rang.

2. WHILE → Dos acciones largas al mismo tiempo
Ejemplo: While Sarah was cooking, Tom was setting the table.

3. AS → Igual que “while”, pero más formal o literario
Ejemplo: As we were walking home, it started to snow.

4. AS SOON AS / THE MOMENT THAT → En cuanto… (acción inmediata)
Ejemplo: As soon as the teacher left, the students started talking.
Ejemplo: The moment that I opened the door, the dog ran out.

5. JUST AS → Justo en el momento en que…
Ejemplo: Just as I was falling asleep, my phone rang.

Resumen visual de los conectores

Resumen rápido (tabla)

Conector Significado Ejemplo
when cuando (interrupción) I was eating when she arrived.
while / as mientras (simultáneo) While I was eating, she was reading.
as soon as en cuanto As soon as I finished, I left.
the moment that en el momento en que The moment that he saw me, he smiled.
just as justo cuando Just as I sat down, the film started.
